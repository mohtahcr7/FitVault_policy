Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction

FitVault ("the App") is a private, fully offline utility application designed to help users store and organize clothing and shoe sizes for themselves and their family members. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information when you use it. By using FitVault, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use immediately.

2. Information We Collect

FitVault does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you voluntarily enter into the App — including names, avatar choices, clothing sizes, size systems, and brand notes — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Hive database).

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or browsing history
  • Biometric or health data
  • Photos, camera, microphone, or media files
  • Any data from third-party services

No data ever leaves your device. There are no servers, APIs, or external network connections used by the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since FitVault does not collect any data, there is no use of your information beyond what you explicitly choose to store locally on your device. All processing happens entirely on-device and remains under your control.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

FitVault does NOT share, sell, rent, or disclose any user data to third parties because no user data is collected or transmitted.

The App integrates the following third-party libraries that operate entirely on-device:

  • Hive — Local NoSQL database
  • Google Fonts — Downloads and caches fonts locally
  • share_plus — Uses Android native share sheet only when initiated by the user

We do NOT use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporting tools, or any external tracking services.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as you keep the App installed.

  • Delete individual size entries anytime
  • Delete entire profiles whenever you want
  • Remove all stored data permanently by uninstalling the App

Since there are no servers, uninstalling the App permanently deletes all information.

6. Data Security

Because all data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally, security depends primarily on your own device protection.

  • Use a screen lock or password
  • Keep Android updated
  • Install apps only from trusted sources

Hive stores data inside the app’s private Android directory, inaccessible to other apps on standard non-rooted devices.

7. Children's Privacy

FitVault is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. The App does NOT collect personal information from any user regardless of age.

  • No data collection from children
  • No targeted advertising
  • No in-app purchases
  • No age verification or tracking

8. User Rights

You always maintain full control over your information.

  • View your stored data
  • Edit or update information anytime
  • Delete entries or profiles whenever you choose
  • Export information manually through the share feature

Since no data is stored remotely, no additional deletion request process is required.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal or functional changes. Any updates will revise the "Last updated" date shown at the top of this page.
